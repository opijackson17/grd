Geothermal manifestation on Earth’s surface that may signify existence of a geothermal system
GEOTHERMAL PROSPECTS IN UGANDA.
  1. BURANGA GEOTHERMAL PROSPECT
    This prospect is located in Bundibugyo District, Bwamba County, and Semuliki National Park. Geothermal manifestations include hot springs, geothermal grass, hydrothermally altered rocks and hydrogen sulphide gas and travertine deposits. The hot springs at Kagoro, Nyansimbe and Mumbuga have recorded a maximum temperature of 98°C.
  2. KIBIRO GEOTHERMAL PROSPECT
    3. Kibiro geothermal prospect is located in Hoima District, western Uganda. There are three hot springs;
    • Mukabiga, temperature 57°C − 86°C
    • Mwibanda, temperature 33°C − 72°C
    • Muntere, temperature 45°C
